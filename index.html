West Side Pride

A Pride Festival for the West Side of Ottawa and the surrounding regions

July 22 2023
12:00pm - 5:00pm

Alexander Grove Park

Come out and join us for the first annual West Side Pride event! We created this event to celebrate and promote the amazing 2SLGBTQIA+ community in the western part of Ottawa and the surrounding areas into the Ottawa Valley.

We're planning an amazing afternoon, rain or shine, with entertainment, an info fair, and a Kids Can area (Kids can enjoy pride too; play area).

West Side Pride is an accessible event, with lots of free parking. Bring your own chairs and picnic lunch or pick up something on site!
