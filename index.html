West Side Pride

The Pride Festival for the West Side of Ottawa and the surrounding regions

July 27th 2024
12:00pm - 5:00pm

Alexander Grove Park

Come out and join us for the second annual West Side Pride event! We created this event to celebrate and promote the amazing 2SLGBTQIA+ community in the western part of Ottawa and the surrounding areas into the Ottawa Valley.

West Side Pride is an accessible event, with lots of free parking. Food and drink are available for purchase on site.

We are looking for volunteers. If you are interested in helping out, please fill out our volunteer form.

We hope to see you all there!
